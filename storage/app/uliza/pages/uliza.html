Uliza is a Swahili word that means “ask”.

This is a clinician’s support platform within the National HIV clinical support Centre (NHCSC), which has 2 arms:

  • Email consultation (ulizanascop@gmail.com)
  • Phone consultation (0800724848).

In the Email consultation, this is where clinicians can send it difficult cases (Treatment failure, comorbidities, adverse drug reactions etc.) for recommendation by the expert Team.

The phone consultation on the hand is where the clinicians are able to call using a toll-free line and make inquiries. The 2 platforms run on a web-based platform that is hosted at NASCOP.
